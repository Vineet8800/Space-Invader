Space Invader User Manual

How To Play


Default Buttons ~~

Shoot : SpaceBar , MouseLeft
Laser shoot : Shift
Pause : Esc

How to change Spaceship Avatar?

To change your Spaceship Avatar, go to the Settings and choose one of the available Spaceships.


How to turn On/Off background music ?

To turn On/Off background music, go to the Settings and click on Music On/Off Button.


How to turn On/Off game sounds ?

To turn On/Off game sounds(shoot,explode), go to the Settings and click on Sound On/Off Button.


How to choose my own background music ?

To choose your own background music, copy the .mp3 file to the Sound folder of the game. Now go to the game Settings and click on "My Own Music" Button. Select your copied file. Your music will automatically starts playing.
